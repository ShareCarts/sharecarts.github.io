How to Share Your Newegg Cart

Last Updated: February 9th, 2025

Newegg is an online retailer specializing in computer hardware, electronics, and gaming products. It’s popular for selling PC components, laptops, gaming peripherals, and DIY PC-building parts like graphics cards, processors, and motherboards.

In this guide, we will walk you through every step needed to share your Newegg cart with anyone!

Step 1: Add items to your Newegg cart Here is an example cart:
  1. Intel Core i7-14700K - Core i7 14th Gen 20-Core (8P+12E) LGA 1700 125W Intel UHD Graphics 770 Processor
  2. AOACE Gaming PC Desktop INTEL Core i5 12400F 2.5 GHz, NVIDIA RTX 4060 8G DLSS 3, 32GB DDR4 RAM 3200MHz,1TB NVMe PCIe4.0, Wi-Fi6E, Game Design Office console,Sea View Room,Windows 11 Home 64-bit
  3. MSI - 15.6" GeForce RTX 4060 Laptop GPU - AMD Ryzen 9 8945HS - 16GB Memory - 1 TB PCIe SSD
  4. Monster Hunter Wilds - PC
  5. Yakuza: Like a Dragon Legendary Hero Edition for PC
Step 2: Construct the share link

For each item in your cart, append this parameter to the URL below: PRODUCT ID|Quantity, ➡️ The PRODUCT ID can be located at the end of the product URL. ➡️ The QUANTITY is the quantity of the product ex. 1.

https://secure.newegg.com/Shopping/AddToCart.aspx?Submit=ADD&ItemList=

Using the example cart from Step 1, the final share URL is:

https://secure.newegg.com/Shopping/AddToCart.aspx?Submit=ADD&ItemList=N82E16819118466|1,N82E16832203394|1,N82E16832233291|1,N82E16834156648|1,9SIBV1MK713275|1
Step 3: Send the link

🔗 Send the link via SMS, email, social media, or anywhere you can paste a link! You could send this to family letting them know what to buy for the holidays.

✅ Rather than sharing product links separately, you can now you can share them together as a single collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the link working?

Occasionally, you may run into hiccups while trying to construct your link. Use this free tool ↗ to generate the correct cart link automatically.

How many items can I send?

There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the share link. It works on all desktop & mobile browsers.

Which locales are supported?

Newegg United States https://newegg.com ↗ and Canada https://newegg.ca ↗ are supported for cart sharing.

Does my Newegg cart link stay updated?

No, links to your Newegg cart don't stay updated. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the same process in this guide to create a new cart link.
