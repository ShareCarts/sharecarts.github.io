How to Share Your B&H Photo Cart

Last Updated: January 14th, 2025

B&H Photo (B&H Photo Video) is a major retailer specializing in photography, video, and audio equipment, as well as computers, drones, and other electronics.

In this guide, we will walk you through every step needed to share your B&H Photo cart with anyone!

Step 1: Add items to your B&H Photo cart Here is an example cart:
  1. JVC GY-HM250 UHD 4K Streaming Camcorder
  2. Ruggard Optica 15 DSLR Roller V2 (Black)
  3. Vello LCD Screen Protector
  4. Sony PCK-LG3 Glass Screen Protector for a7R V and a9 III Sony Mirrorless Cameras
  5. Vortex Binocular Harness Strap
Step 2: Construct the share link

For each item in your cart, append this parameter to the URL below: SKU:REG:Quantity| ➡️ The SKU can be located in the product URL. ➡️ The QUANTITY is the quantity of the product ex. 1.

https://www.bhphotovideo.com/bnh/controller/home?O=getpage.jsp&A=cart&Q=add&skus=

Using the example cart from Step 1, the final share URL is:

https://www.bhphotovideo.com/bnh/controller/home?O=getpage.jsp&A=cart&Q=add&skus=436591:REG:1|1734610:REG:1|1653717:REG:1|1416029:REG:1|1400279:REG:1
Step 3: Send the link

🔗 Send the link via SMS, email, social media, or anywhere you can paste a link! You could send this to a friend letting them know what to buy for your birthday.

✅ Rather than sharing product links separately, you can now you can share them together as a single collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the link working?

Occasionally, you may run into hiccups while trying to construct your link. Use this free tool ↗ to generate the correct cart link automatically.

How many items can I send?

There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the share link. It works on all desktop & mobile browsers.

Which locales are supported?

B&H Photo operates in the United States, so only https://bhphotovideo.com ↗ is supported for cart sharing.

Does my B&H Photo cart link stay updated?

No, links to your B&H Photo cart don't stay updated. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the same process in this guide to create a new cart link.
