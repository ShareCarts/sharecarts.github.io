How to Share Your IKEA Cart

Last Updated: March 4th, 2025

IKEA is a multinational company known for its affordable, ready-to-assemble furniture, home accessories, and kitchen appliances.

In this guide, we will walk you through every step needed to share your IKEA cart with anyone!

Step 1: Add items to your IKEA cart Here is an example cart:
  1. Clothes rack
  2. Cushion cover
  3. Rug flatwoven
  4. Table and 2 folding chairs
  5. Pizza cutter
Step 2: Construct the share link

For each item in your cart, append this parameter to the URL below: PRODUCT ID:Quantity, ➡️ The PRODUCT ID can be located at the end of the product URL. ➡️ The QUANTITY is the quantity of the product ex. 1.

https://www.ikea.com/us/en/favorites/receive-share/

Using the example cart from Step 1, the final share URL is:

https://www.ikea.com/us/en/favorites/receive-share/60179434:1,60581312:1,50495409:1,09573018:1,20578260:1
Step 3: Send the link

🔗 Send the link via SMS, email, social media, or anywhere you can paste a link! You could send this to a roommate letting them know what to buy for the new living space.

✅ Rather than sharing product links separately, you can now you can share them together as a single collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the link working?

Occasionally, you may run into hiccups while trying to construct your link. Use this free tool ↗ to generate the correct cart link automatically.

How many items can I send?

There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the share link. It works on all desktop & mobile browsers.

Which locales are supported?

IKEA operates in more than 60 countries across Europe, North America, Asia, the Middle East, and Oceania. All of these countries are supported for cart sharing.

Does my B&H Photo cart link stay updated?

No, links to your IKEA cart don't stay updated. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the same process in this guide to create a new cart link.
