How to Export Amazon Cart

Exporting your Amazon cart can be useful in many different ways. For starters, it makes sharing your shopping list much easier. Instead of sending people a bunch of links or screenshots, you can just send them a clean, organized file that shows everything in your cart. This is great when you're planning a group purchase, buying gifts for someone, or working on a team project where others need to see or approve what you’re getting.

One of the best ways is by using a free browser tool like the Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗. It helps you organize, share, and export your cart in just a few clicks. In this article, we’ll explain why someone might want to export their Amazon cart and how this tool makes the process easy.

Why Export Your Amazon Cart?

  • To Use for Work or Business – If you buy items for your job, school, or business, a CSV file makes things easy to report, reorder, or file for expenses. You can add extra columns for order dates, budget codes, or who the items are for.
  • To Save Your Cart for Later – Amazon doesn’t always keep items in your cart forever. Products can go out of stock, prices can change, or you might accidentally remove something. Exporting your cart helps you save a backup. That way, even if something changes on Amazon, you still have a list of what you planned to buy.
  • To Organize and Compare Items – A CSV file opens in programs like Excel or Google Sheets. You can sort by price, group similar products, or add your own notes (like “Need by Friday” or “Check reviews”). This makes it easier to choose between options or stick to a budget.

Can You Export Your Cart on Amazon?

Yes! You can either:

  • Use the extension Share Amazon Cart ↗
  • Copy and paste each product seperately into a spreadsheet.

Amazon doesn’t have a “export cart” button, but you can still do it by hand.

The Best Way: Use the "Share Amazon Cart" Extension

The best way to export your cart is to use the Chrome Extension ↗. Normally, when you want to export a cart, you need to do it manually. This tool exports all your cart items in one click, to CSV format.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Add items to your Amazon cart like usual.
  3. Open your cart and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Click "Export Cart".
