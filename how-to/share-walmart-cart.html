How to Share Your Walmart Cart

Last Updated: January 29th, 2025

Walmart is one of the largest retail chains in the world, offering a wide range of products, including groceries, electronics, clothing, home goods, and more.

In this guide, we will walk you through every step needed to share your Walmart cart with anyone!

Step 1: Add items to your Walmart cart Here is an example cart:
  1. Impossible Foods Chicken Nuggets Meat from Plants, Frozen, Fully Cooked, 13.5 oz
  2. Steak-umm Sliced Steaks All-Natural 100% Beef, 12 oz 8 Count, (Frozen)
  3. Philadelphia Original Cream Cheese Spread, 16 oz Tub
  4. Waterloo Sparkling Water, Peach, 12 fl oz, 8 pack cans
  5. Binshrliy Newborn Baby Boys Fall Rompers Shirts Long Sleeve Plaid Bodysuits 3M 6M 12M 18M Infant One Piece Jumpsuit
Step 2: Construct the share link

For each item in your cart, append this parameter to the URL below: SKU|Quantity, ➡️ The SKU can be located in the product URL. ➡️ The QUANTITY is the quantity of the product ex. 1.

https://affil.walmart.com/cart/addToCart?offers=

Using the example cart from Step 1, the final share URL is:

https://affil.walmart.com/cart/addToCart?offers=3BB5D716F3A14CABBAE03BA5DCAFFE65|1,D7223BBCBAF230B3932FC292B630AFE0|1,7A20E534D5B2444CBD6828B1414F5495|1,470E185061213F17B8A720527466E0BC|1,BFD7D3FDA3AC3DAAA98566D3DE8D1B92|1
Step 3: Send the link

🔗 Send the link via SMS, email, social media, or anywhere you can paste a link! You could send this to roommates to split a grocery shopping list.

✅ Rather than sharing product links separately, you can now you can share them together as a single collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the link working?

Occasionally, you may run into hiccups while trying to construct your link. Use this free tool ↗ to generate the correct cart link automatically.

How many items can I send?

There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the share link. It works on all desktop & mobile browsers.

Which locales are supported?

Walmart United States https://walmart.com ↗ and Business https://business.walmart.com ↗ are supported for cart sharing.

Does my Walmart cart link stay updated?

No, links to your Walmart cart don't stay updated. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the same process in this guide to create a new cart link.
