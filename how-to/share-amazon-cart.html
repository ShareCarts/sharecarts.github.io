How to Share Your Amazon Cart

Last Updated: March 25th, 2025

Amazon is the world's largest online retailer, offering everything from electronics and clothing to groceries and digital services. It is common to ask others for shopping opinions, or even send friends your entire cart.

Learn how to share your Amazon cart in a few easy steps.

The following questions are answered:
  • Is there a way to share my Amazon cart?
  • How to share Amazon cart with someone else?
  • How can I share my entire Amazon cart?

Step 2: Make The Link

For each item in your cart, append this parameter to the URL below: ASIN.#=ASIN&Quantity.#=QUANTITY& ➡️ The # is the number it is in the cart. Starting at 1. ➡️ The ASIN can be located under "Product information" on the product page. ➡️ The QUANTITY is the quantity of the product ex. 1.

https://www.amazon.com/gp/aws/cart/add.html?

Using the example cart from Step 1, the final share URL is:

https://www.amazon.com/gp/aws/cart/add.html?ASIN.1=B0B94J7ZM7&Quantity.1=1&ASIN.2=B01D0Q7JU4&Quantity.2=1&ASIN.3=B07NCNRCMV&Quantity.3=1&ASIN.4=B0D9LCL7FT&Quantity.4=1&ASIN.5=B09TSRBS47&Quantity.5=1&AssociateTag=sharecarts0c-20

This one link contains all the items ready for purchase without screenshots or wishlists.

Exact items are shared: The same size or colors will be applied to the recipient cart.

Exact quantities are shared: 1 or more Apple Pencil Tips (4 pack) can be included.

Step 3: Share a Cart

🔗 Send the link via SMS, email, social media, or anywhere you can paste a link! You could send a required school supplies list to your students, or share a cart with family.

👨‍👩‍👦 Another person receives the link, such as: friends, family, or co-workers.

✅ Rather than sharing product links separately, you can now you can share them together as a single collection.

Note: When the recipient clicks 'Add To Cart', it won't clear the contents of their existing cart. So anything there previously will remain.
