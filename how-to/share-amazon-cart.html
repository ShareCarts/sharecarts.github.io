How to Share Your Amazon Cart

Last Updated: March 7th, 2025

Amazon is the world's largest online retailer, offering everything from electronics and clothing to groceries and digital services.

In this guide, we will walk you through every step needed to share your Amazon cart with anyone!

Step 1: Add items to your Amazon cart Here is an example cart:
  1. Magnetic Picture Frame 12 Pack 2x3 Picture Frame Mini Picture Frames Set For Polaroid Frame
  2. Motivational Stickers - Vinyl Inspirational Stickers for Water Bottle Skateboard Laptop
  3. Bulk Toothbrush Pack with Covers
  4. Fujifilm Instax Mini Instant Film
  5. Instax Mini 2x3 Polaroid Photo Acrylic Frame

As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.

Step 2: Construct the share link

For each item in your cart, append this parameter to the URL below: ASIN.#=PRODUCT ID&Quantity.#=QUANTITY& ➡️ The # is the number it is in the cart. Starting at 1. ➡️ The PRODUCT ID can be located in the product URL. ➡️ The QUANTITY is the quantity of the product ex. 1.

https://www.amazon.com/gp/aws/cart/add.html?

Using the example cart from Step 1, the final share URL is:

https://www.amazon.com/gp/aws/cart/add.html?ASIN.1=B0B94J7ZM7&Quantity.1=1&ASIN.2=B01D0Q7JU4&Quantity.2=1&ASIN.3=B07NCNRCMV&Quantity.3=1&ASIN.4=B0D9LCL7FT&Quantity.4=1&ASIN.5=B09TSRBS47&Quantity.5=1&AssociateTag=sharecarts0c-20
Step 3: Send the link

🔗 Send the link via SMS, email, social media, or anywhere you can paste a link! You could send a required school supplies list to your students.

✅ Rather than sharing product links separately, you can now you can share them together as a single collection.
