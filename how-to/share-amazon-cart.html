How to Share Amazon Cart

Amazon is the world's largest online retailer. You can buy almost anything—clothes, electronics, books, groceries, and more. But what if you want to show someone else what you’re planning to buy? Maybe you're shopping with a friend, planning a gift with your partner, or asking someone to check your cart. That’s where cart sharing comes in.

Even though Amazon doesn't have a built-in way to share your cart, there are simple ways to do it. In this guide, you’ll learn how to share your Amazon cart, including how to use a Chrome Extension ↗ to make it fast and easy.

Why Share Your Amazon Cart?

Sharing your Amazon cart makes shopping with others much easier. It’s useful when you’re buying gifts for a friend or planning a big family order. Imagine coordinating a birthday present, a holiday shopping list, or even stocking up on household items with multiple people. Instead of sending many separate links, you can simply share one cart. This way, everyone sees the same items, can suggest changes, or copy the cart if they want to buy the same things.

It saves time and reduces mistakes, like forgetting something important or accidentally duplicating items. Sharing your cart also helps with organizing group orders at work or planning a special event, making the process smoother and more efficient.

Can You Share Your Cart on Amazon?

Yes! You can either:

  • Use the extension Share Amazon Cart ↗
  • Move items to a wishlist and share that (only if signed in)
  • Copy and paste links to individual items

The Best Way: Use the "Share Amazon Cart" Extension

The best way to share your cart is to use the Share Amazon Cart Chrome Extension ↗. This tool lets you turn your cart into a simple link that you can send to anyone.

What Is the Share Amazon Cart Extension?

This is a free tool made for Chrome / Edge users. It creates a link that includes all the items in your cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see the same items and can add them to their own cart. It works across different Amazon regions and doesn’t need your Amazon login or password.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Add items to your Amazon cart like usual.
  3. Open your cart and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.
  5. The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • No need to copy every product link
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • Shares exact items and quantities
  • Shares exact colors and sizes

Manual Process

  1. Open each item in the browser.
  2. Copy the URL from your browser.
  3. Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not efficient for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I share my grocery items?
Yes! In addition to the main Amazon website, carts on Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods can be shared.
Do I need to be signed into my Amazon account?
No, without signing in, you can still share carts with the extension.
Can I transfer my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to Send Amazon Cart or Transfer Amazon Cart to another account.
Will the other person see my address or payment info?
No! The cart link only shows the item names and prices. Your name, address, and credit card info stay private.
Which locales are supported?
Amazon operates across 5 continents with around 20 countries having dedicated websites for shoppers and sellers. All the steps and examples in this guide apply to every Amazon website worldwide, like: https://amazon.com ↗, https://www.amazon.ca ↗, https://www.amazon.fr ↗, https://www.amazon.de ↗, https://www.amazon.it ↗, https://www.amazon.co.uk ↗.

Some regional restrictions or differences in product availability might affect what the recipient can see or buy.

How many items can I send?
There is no limit to how many different items you can send using the extension. In some cases, Amazon may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items. It works on all desktop & mobile browsers.
Will the links I create expire?
No, the links stay active indefinitely. However, product availability may vary.
Does my Amazon cart link stay updated?
No, if you change the items in your cart, you should use the same process in this guide to create a new link.

Conclusion

Sharing your Amazon cart can be really helpful. Whether you’re buying gifts, planning events, or just shopping together, it saves time and avoids confusion. The best way to do it is by using the Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗. It’s fast, safe, and easy to use. If that’s not an option, wishlists and copy-pasting still work too.
