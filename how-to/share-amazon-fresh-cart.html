How to Share Amazon Fresh Cart

Amazon Fresh makes grocery shopping super easy. You pick out your groceries online, schedule delivery, and get everything sent straight to your door. It’s fast, simple, and really convenient. But sometimes, you may want to share your Amazon Fresh cart with someone else. Maybe you’re planning meals with a roommate, splitting grocery duties with a partner, or just showing someone your list. Whatever the reason, sharing your Amazon Fresh cart can be a big help.

In this guide, we’ll show you how to share your Amazon Fresh cart. We’ll also walk through using the Share Amazon Cart Chrome extension ↗, which is the easiest way to share your Amazon Fresh items.

Why Share Your Amazon Fresh Cart?

Sharing your Amazon Fresh grocery cart is a smart and easy way to shop together with others. It helps save time, reduce mistakes, and keep everything more organized. There are many common situations where this helps. Imagine planning a big dinner and needing to check with your partner before ordering. Or two coworkers organizing snacks and drinks for a work event. Even parents helping their kids at college shop for weekly meals. All of these tasks become easier when everyone can see the same cart.

Can You Share Your Amazon Fresh Cart?

Yes! Even though Amazon doesn’t have a built-in “share cart” feature, it is still possible.

The best way to do it is by using a Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool helps you turn your cart into a link that you can share with anyone. There are also other ways like using wishlists or copying product links, but the Chrome extension is the most straightforward way on Amazon Fresh.

Using the “Share Amazon Cart” Chrome Extension

What It Is

The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ is a free tool for Chrome and Edge users. It lets you create a link from your Amazon cart. This link includes everything in your cart—even Amazon Fresh items. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see all the items and can add them to their own cart with one click.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

Why It Works for Amazon Fresh

Amazon Fresh is part of the main Amazon website. So when you add groceries to your Fresh cart, they appear in your normal cart too. That’s why this extension works—it reads your cart and builds a shareable link to it, including your groceries.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Add items to your Amazon cart like usual.
  3. Open your cart and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.
  5. The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • No need to copy every product link
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • Shares exact items and quantities
  • Shares exact brands and sizes

    • Paste each link into a message.

    This works if you only have a few products. It’s not ideal for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I share my Fresh cart with someone in another city?
    Yes, but they may not be able to buy the same items. Amazon Fresh is only available in certain zip codes. Also, some products—like deli meats or prepared foods—might be local only.
    What happens if items go out of stock?
    Amazon Fresh stocks can change quickly. If you share a cart and someone clicks the link later, some items might be gone or replaced. It’s best to double-check everything before they order.
    Can I use this for Whole Foods too?
    Yes! If you order Whole Foods through Amazon, the extension can help share that cart as well. It works with most groceries as long as they’re in your Amazon shopping cart.
