Frequently Asked Questions

What is a share cart link?
A share cart link is a URL that allows someone to view (and sometimes purchase) the exact items that are in a shopping cart. It is perfect for anything that requires sending multiple products. By making sure the recipient receives the exact cart items and quantities, there is never confusion about what items need to be purchased.

Can I share my Amazon cart?
Yes! Install the Chrome or Edge browser extension to share in one click. It's free, super light-weight, and only activates when you are on a supported website. By using the Share Amazon Cart Extension, you can avoid the manual process of sending someone all your cart items separately.
Who can I share my cart with?
  • Family - You can share your Amazon cart with your family. This includes your mom, dad, sister, brother, or anyone else in your household.
  • Friends - You can also share a cart with your friends.
  • Roommates - Sharing your cart with a roommate or housemate can help everyone stay organized.
  • Your Partner - Do you live with a boyfriend, girlfriend, or spouse? Cart sharing with your partner can make life simpler.
  • Coworkers - Sometimes, you shop for work. In those cases, sharing your Amazon cart with coworkers or team members is useful.
  • Clients or Customers - If you have a small business or do freelance work, you might need to share your Amazon cart with a client.
  • Online Communities - You might also want to share a cart with online groups — like Reddit, Facebook groups, Discord, or forums.
What are common use cases for sharing a cart?
  • Holiday Shopping - Plan and share holiday shopping carts for birthdays, Christmas, or other celebrations to avoid overlap and stay within budget.
  • Collaborative gift buying - Share a cart among friends or family for shopping trips, allowing everyone to add and review items before finalizing the purchase.
  • Team or Office Purchases - Share a cart to purchase office supplies, equipment, or team uniforms, making bulk buying and cost-sharing easier.
  • Grocery Shopping - Family members can share a cart for home essentials, groceries, or furniture, ensuring the correct items are bought and budgeted.
  • Event Planning - Plan and share carts for weddings, parties, or gatherings, where multiple people contribute to buying decorations, supplies, or gifts.
Where else can I share my cart?
In addition to Amazon, share your cart on top online stores such as: Walmart, Newegg, Instacart, and more. Browse our in-depth guides on how to generate these cart links. Stores powered by Shopify are also supported, which adds millions of additional stores.
What are the differences between a wishlist and share cart links?
Currently, it is not possible to move all items from an Amazon wishlist to a shopping cart easily. Another downside of wishlists is it doesn't support moving the exact quantities to a cart. Share Amazon Cart Extension provides solutions to both these issues, making it a great alternative to using a traditional wishlist.
