❓Frequently Asked Questions

What is a share cart link?

It is one simple link that contains all your cart items. Perfect for anything that requires sending multiple products. Most common use cases: group gift buying, school supplies, office purchasing, grocery shopping, and holiday shopping

For example, you can share your Amazon cart with family to help them pick out holiday gifts. By making sure the recipient receives the exact cart items, there is never confusion about what items need to be purchased.

Who can I send my cart link to?

With the help of our guides, you can share your cart with friends, family, and co-workers! There is no limitation who you can share a cart with. Keep in mind: if you make changes to your shopping cart, generate a new link to share the updated items.

What are the differences between a wishlist and sharing a cart directly?

Currently, it is not possible to move all items from an Amazon wishlist to a shopping cart easily. With Share Carts you can send someone your entire shopping cart directly, removing the hassle of manual transfer. Another downside of wishlists is it doesn't support moving the exact quantities to a cart. Share Carts solves both these issues, making it a great alternative to using a traditional wishlist.
