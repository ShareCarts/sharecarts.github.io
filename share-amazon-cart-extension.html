​The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ simplifies the process of sharing your online shopping carts from various retailers, including Amazon. By generating a single, shareable link, it's more efficient than traditional wishlists or screenshots.

Why Use Share Amazon Cart Extension?

Normally, if you want to share what’s in your cart, you’d have to copy each item link one by one or take a bunch of screenshots. That takes time, and it’s easy to miss something. With this extension ↗, you just click one button, and boom — you’ve got a clean, clickable link that shows your whole cart. It's faster, easier, and you won’t forget anything.

Here’s a few reasons why people love it:

Saves Time on Group Orders - Let’s say you're ordering supplies for an event, classroom, or office. You’ve got everything picked out, but now you need someone else to look it over, approve it, or buy it. Instead of sending a long list of items, you just share the cart link. The other person can see all the products and even buy them straight from the link.

Works Great for Gift Planning - Around birthdays or holidays, families and friend groups often plan gifts together. With this tool, one person can create a cart with ideas and send it to the others. Everyone can agree on what to buy — or even split the order. It takes the guesswork out of group gifting.

No Confusion About Products - Sometimes, people send each other product names, but they end up buying the wrong version, size, or brand. With a cart link, there’s no confusion. The recipient sees the exact items you picked, straight from Amazon. That means fewer mistakes and no awkward returns.

How It Works

  1. Install the Extension - Available on Chrome ↗ or Edge ↗ browsers.
  2. Add Items to Your Cart - Navigate to Amazon and add items to your shopping cart as usual.​
  3. Generate a Shareable Link - After adding items, click the Share Carts icon in your browser's toolbar.​ The extension will automatically create one shareable link for your cart.
  4. Share the Cart - Copy the generated link.​ Share the link via email, messaging apps, or any preferred method.

Key Features

  • Universal Sharing: This extension is compatible with all Amazon regions, including Amazon.com, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods. It also supports other major retailers such as: Walmart, Best Buy, IKEA, Instacart, and Newegg.
  • Editable: Modify the items in your cart before sharing, including quantities and product selections.​
  • Add Note: Add a note or instructions for the recipient.
  • Export: Export Your cart to a CSV file, including: product titles, URLs, quantities, and prices.
  • Print: Print Your Cart, removes all the clutter, and just prints out the relevant product information.
  • Additional Sharing Options: Generate a QR code for easy mobile access and share your cart via various platforms.

Additional Notes

  • The recipient does not need the extension installed to view or load the cart.
  • The cart contents will be loaded into the recipient’s own Amazon account, giving them full control over purchase decisions.
  • Generated links do not expire and remain accessible.
  • No registration is required, and your personal account information is not shared.
