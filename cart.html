What happens when I click 'Continue to Amazon'?

It will take you to a confirmation page on Amazon.

Click the 'Add To Cart' button.

It does not remove any items already in your cart.

Does this cart link stay updated?

No, the link reflects all cart items at the time it was created. To make changes, use the extension to create a new link.

Can I share my groceries?

Yes, in addition to the main Amazon website, carts from Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods can also be shared. Use the extension to create a grocery cart link.
