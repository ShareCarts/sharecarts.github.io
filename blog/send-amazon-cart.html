There are many reasons to send your Amazon cart to someone, such as: group gift buying, gift suggestions, grocery lists, and more. If you're looking to do either, or have another use case, you're in luck. It is very easy to include all your cart items with one link.

Instead of sending each item link seperately, it is better to send one pre-filled cart link.

Using this method - there is no confusion on items to purchase, and the recipient receives the entire cart.

The cart link can be sent to anyone, make sure to only include items you wish to share.

Note: When the recipient clicks 'Add To Cart', it won't clear the contents of their existing cart. So anything there previously will remain.
